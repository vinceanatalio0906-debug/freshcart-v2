Who We Are

FreshCart is a specialized hardware e-commerce platform developed as a **System Project** by three dedicated students from **Valenzuela City**. Our mission is to provide an accessible and reliable way for gamers and general users to find the best PC components without the hassle.

Driven by a passion for technology, we built this system to showcase the balance between performance and affordability. From entry-level builds to enthusiast setups, we believe everyone deserves quality hardware.

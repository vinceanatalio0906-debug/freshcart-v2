About FreshCart

A marketplace system project by Valenzuela's builders

Who We Are

FreshCart is a flexible e-commerce marketplace developed as a system project by three dedicated students from Valenzuela City. Our mission is to provide an accessible and reliable way for buyers to discover everyday products from trusted sellers.

Driven by a passion for practical online shopping, we built this system to showcase a smoother way to browse products, compare prices, monitor stock, and support small sellers.
